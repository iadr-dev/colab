# Product brief stub (optional, early framing)

Fill only what matters for engineering. Omit sections that duplicate an existing PRD.

## Problem
Who hurts, how, and frequency?

## Outcome
Observable **done**: what changes for the user or system?

## Non-goals
Explicit exclusions to prevent scope creep.

## Constraints
Hard (compliance, SLAs), soft (timeline, staffing).

## Risks / unknowns
What could invalidate the approach?

## Success signals
Signals that map to acceptance tests or verifier checks (not vibes).
